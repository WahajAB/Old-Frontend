Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of documents can I use?

PharmaBot supports a wide range of pharmaceutical documents including clinical trial reports, regulatory submissions, market research, patent filings, and scientific publications in PDF, Word, and Excel formats.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use enterprise-grade encryption and comply with HIPAA, GDPR, and other relevant data protection regulations. Your documents are processed securely and never stored permanently on our servers.

Which countries are the documents from?

Our AI is trained on pharmaceutical data from major markets including the US, EU, Japan, Canada, and Australia. We support documents in multiple languages and regulatory frameworks.
